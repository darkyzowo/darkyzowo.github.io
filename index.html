How to play?

Try to guess the word within 6 tries!


  • Each guess must be a valid & existing 5 letter word.

  • With each guess, the colors of the tiles will change, this will reveal helpful information.

  • If you manage to guess the word within 6 tries, a fancy animation will play & all the tiles will turn green.

  • However, if you do not manage to guess the word within 6 tries, you'll be presented with a "nice try, better luck next time" support message. But don't worry, you can always try again tomorrow (word resets every 24 hrs!)
